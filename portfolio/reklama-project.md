# How find the thing for OOH advertisement company

Or how to survive at COVID-19 if you are small team among big competitors.

## Challenge

What the connection betwee COVID 19 and Blender 2.8 release? Probably only by one thing - without both of them this story probably would never happened.

While big companies OOH advertisement was always competitive, small teams like ours usally worked with grocery stores, barbershops and shawarma kiosks. And it's actually great small businesses. But if something unexpected happens, like pandemy, small business it's not a type of client you should rely to. But we did.

-- Infographic about number of business that closes in 2020

It was early 2020 when local businesses we worked with start massively closing and stoping their work. We was needed rapidly find new clients and projects to save ourselves and business. We already did our first steps working with big pharmacy stores chain, and it was obvious that if we got more such clients, it would allow us not only survive but even grow.

-- Infographic how much big companies rise during Covid

But not we only was so clever, so niche that before was crowded by mature manufactoring companies who inertyle compete for tenders get freesh breeze with young companies, who suggest same product but cheaper, more, faster, and so on and so forth.

We decide also take part in this race and above very proffessional team and competitive price we also make a lot to present each project to customer in new, inconvinient and much more effective way.

## How make it not just differen, but better?

To understand the solution we came up with, it's important to mention Blender 2.8 was released шт еру summer of 2019 and open ability to create 3d graphic faster and render it in realtime. I had skills, and decide to test if I can recreate one of our object with 3d and project all add constuctions on it. And result was not only much better, it was also faster and make all montage works much more predictable, due to precice work with sizes.

## Process outline

1. Process started from measuring object. Nothing special, just key metrics of object and one-two objects near it
2. Then we recreate this oject with block model of an object- such model usually take 10-20 minutes. we already can try to apply draft ads constructions on it
3. Internal discussion and project planning
4. Elaboration of details with textures, a bit more detailed block out and applying clean ads constructions - 1-2 hours of time depending on object complexity
5. Creation of presentation higlightin key points

## Results

1. Switching from small to middle-to-large projects
2. Standing out from competitors with unique technology
3. Business expansion and new oportunities

## Conclusion

It may seems surprising for someone designer so care about business side of job, because it's a lot about strategy. But since I'm marketing focus designer - for me important to know not only how we look like, but also what we sell, whom we sale and why they should choose us among competitors. This case shows what results could bring such collaboration between business, marketing and design.
