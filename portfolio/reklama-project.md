# How find the thing for OOH advertisement company

Or how to do just a little bit more and succeed

## Challenge

Out of house advertisement manufactoring in small cities is pretty competitive. There is nothing wrong to work with regulary opening shawarma kiosks and small sores, while everything goes as usual. But sometimes happen thing like Covid. That's when we wind ourselves when I worked with Reklama Project. All our customers are stopped or freeze their activity, and company need find new market. This market big brands comissions, becouse thy have continued their work as usual. But problem was they work with same companies all the time even if they make a tender, suprisingly only particular companies win them.
To compete in such situation we decide try to do
a) Cheaper prices
b) More engaging presentation
c) Best services

It's hard to make best service cheaper than normal, right? If this happening, likely some technology stay behind it. This case is not exclusion - Lately Blender 3.0 was came up, I alredy worked with it on game studio where I worked before and I got interest to realtime graphics.

This techonology gives us signivficant advantage comparing major manufactorers who rely on just primitive photopshop manipulation in their work, we have ability to show our work in 360deg, make more precice Calculation and inspire customer much much more. With cheaper price it create amzing effect. Beside thar eacg project add value to our portfolio.

Also, considering repetition it become easier and easier to create new visuals whith adding new assets to our library.

## Process outline

1. So process started from measuring object. Nothing special, just key metrics of object and one-two objects near it
2. Then we recreate this oject with block model of an object- such model usually take 10-20 minutes. we already can try to apply draft ads constructions on it
3. Internal discussion and project planning
4. Elaboration of details with textures, a bit more detailed block out and applying clean ads constructions - 1-2 hours of time depending on object complexity
