Content Strategy for Electronics Brand

The Customer and My Role

Social Media is a significant source of traffic for a business. Proper content strategy is crucial to succeed on social media. Many brands often hire marketing agencies to create an effective strategy. The Official Distributor of Apple Devices came to the agency where I worked for this reason. As Creative Director, my role was to establish content production for CIS countries.

Photo by @salimkerey

Content Strategy and Goals

The gateway to the Apple ecosystem? It's none other than the iPhone. This device is a powerhouse for Apple's sales, with its standout feature being its camera – you might have heard that Instagram even reduces the quality of content uploaded from non-Apple devices. Many aspiring social media enthusiasts invest in an iPhone for its ability to produce high-quality content. Recognizing this trend, Apple created the hashtag #shotoniphone, which showcases amazing photos taken by iPhone users worldwide. This strategy falls under User Generated Content Marketing (UGC Marketing), where users create and share content to boost brand awareness.

Our idea was to leverage this hashtag with regional suffixes, enabling us to gather content from local creators. By publishing this content on our account, we could tailor our channel to a local audience, effectively delivering messages about offers and discounts that drive sales.

Running a UGC campaign requires warming up the audience first, demonstrating how it works. Our internal objective was to create semi-UGC content in collaboration with local creators to promote our hashtag and establish content guidelines for future real UGC content. Our business goal was to generate engaging content that increases brand awareness.

Pre-made Content Workflow

Finding mobile photography enthusiasts on Instagram is not difficult. To build a comprehensive content library for continuous publication, I reached out to them to discuss the possibility of featuring their content on our channel.

Fortunately, most content creators were eager to share their work either for free or in exchange for a small bonus. They saw it as an opportunity to extend their reach, while we benefited from a steady stream of content. The fair deal.

To accelerate the growth of our content creator network, I encouraged creators to refer their fellow iPhone photographers. This not only saved time but also provided valuable insights into the unique dynamics of the local creative scene.

However, there was a limit to the availability of ready-made content. To showcase the full range of iPhone camera capabilities, we needed to produce brand-new semi-UGC content.

Photo by @abenov_mob

Brand New Content Workflow

To generate new content, I collaborated with the same content creators. This approach offered several benefits – I was familiar with their work and expertise, and they were already familiar with our content guidelines.

The workflow proceeded seamlessly: I created a technical brief, curated a mood board, selected the creator, discussed the shoot details, finalized the budget, and supervised the creator throughout production.

Despite being distant from the shooting locations, the most challenging aspect was accommodating real-life factors such as weather conditions and location accessibility. Therefore, I extend a heartfelt thank you to all the creators I worked with for their autonomy and professionalism.

As you can see, our average metrics were nearly nine times higher than the industry average, indicating significant audience engagement. This, coupled with efficient production processes, allowed us to allocate resources towards experimenting with content to further enhance channel performance.

While Engagement Rate provides valuable insights, it's not the sole metric for assessing the effectiveness of a social media channel. It needs to be supported by conversion metrics that show how audience behavior aligns with our objectives. Nonetheless, ER serves as a reliable indicator, particularly in the early stages of a channel's development, highlighting the likelihood of delivering marketing messages effectively. With the right tools, we can fine-tune our messaging to achieve desired conversions and meet our business goals.
