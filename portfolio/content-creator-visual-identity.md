# Visual Identity for Content Creator

## Client

TOYIN is a content creator who animates memorable social media campaigns for products through character creation, narration, photography, and videography.  Her signature is African braiding techniques that she uses for creating distinguish haircuts for every character.

Her target audience is creative directors, fashion lovers, and African culture enthusiasts around the globe and her business goal is new collaborations with brands.

## Goals of project and KPI

Develop an easy-to-apply and captivating visual Identity that reflects TOYIN product and personality.  So like in every personal project, the key performance indicator is “Client is Happy”

![connect-of-two-persnalities.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/connect-of-two-persnalities.jpg)

## Connect of two personalities

We decided to focus all identity on TOYIN's personality, which is rooted in the childhood that she spent in Benin and Nigeria.  This approach perfectly lays on my love for African Culture, folk arts, and the way how they combine with modern culture.

![colors-and-textures.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/colors-and-textures.jpg)

## Creative Guideline

### Colors and textures

I wanted to showcase the aesthetic of each particular case, so I created a cohesive color palette based on a mix of bright and muted shades. Additionally, I added a noisy texture to give each design element its own uniqueness.

![illustrations.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/illustrations.jpg)

### Illustrations

Illustrations use clipping photos and abstract design elements that create a collage effect that underlines doing by hands and soul aesthetic of every TOYIN character. For more uniqueness, we also added a speech bubbles as way to give illustrations more ability to speak.

![titles-and-fonts.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/titles-and-fonts.jpg)

### Titles

This hand-drawn font, inspired by Lilita One, emphasizes the handmade and soulful quality of the text. Scratches along the character silhouette add to the overall aesthetic.

![covers.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/covers.jpg)

### Covers

Covers combine Illustrations, and titles and follow general color guidelines. Every cover is like a poster of an underground band that adds consistency, but storytelling and color mix add every illustration a special mood and helps effectively communicate message of every publication or chapter

![layouts.jpg](/static/images/portfolio/visual-identity/layouts.jpg)

### Layouts

I have used magazine layouts to underline that TOYIN projects are about fashion first of all. Fashion was born in magazines, or at least highly connected with them, so we communicate the message even through layout here. But to outstand from other fashion enthusiasts we use typewriter font, that are almost anti-fashion, countercultural and extremely captivating
