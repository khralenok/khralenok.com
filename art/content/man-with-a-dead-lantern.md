# Man with a dead lantern

## 2019

This illustration shows the man going through the city jungle with a dead lantern in his hands. The narration provides the metaphor of self-discovery in a haze without any saving light that marks the right direction. I have drawn this illustration as a part of the City of Canals project - one of my fiction worlds that my art focuses on.
