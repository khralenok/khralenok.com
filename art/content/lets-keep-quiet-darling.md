# Let's keep quiet, darling

## Let's keep quiet, darling - 2019

The illustration portrays contrasting spouses in the kitchen, a familiar scene repeated each morning. His heart is veiled by formal attire, while hers remains open and vulnerable. She, youthful and vibrant, emanates vitality amidst the tension of their relationship. He, aged and world-weary, seems to blend into the mundane backdrop of everyday life. The artwork encapsulates the ambivalent nature of their incompatibility.
